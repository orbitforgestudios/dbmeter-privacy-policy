Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how DB Meter ("the app") handles user information.

Microphone Usage

The app uses your device’s microphone to measure environmental sound levels (decibels).

  • Audio is processed in real time on your device
  • The app does not record, store, or transmit audio
  • No sound data is shared with any third party

Information We Collect

The app itself does not directly collect or store personal data.

Advertising (AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device information (e.g., device type, OS version)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address (for general location estimation)
  • App interaction data

This data is handled by Google according to their privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

The app may use basic analytics tools (such as Google services) to understand app performance and improve user experience. These services may collect:

  • App usage data
  • Crash logs
  • Device and performance information

How We Use Information

  • To display ads via AdMob
  • To improve app stability and performance

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data may be shared with third-party services (such as Google AdMob) only for advertising and analytics purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled through third-party services.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and above. It is not directed toward children under 13.

User Control

Users can control ad personalization through their device settings (e.g., Ads Settings in Google).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page.
