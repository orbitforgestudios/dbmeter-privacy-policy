Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the application DB Meter ("the app") handles user information.

Microphone Usage

The app uses your device’s microphone to measure environmental sound levels (decibels).

  • Audio is processed in real-time only
  • No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • No sound data leaves your device

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

  • No user registration
  • No collection of personal information
  • No access to contacts, photos, or files
  • No device identifiers collected

How We Use Information

The app does not collect or use any personal data. All sound measurements are processed locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Data Security

Since no user data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data exposure from this app.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
